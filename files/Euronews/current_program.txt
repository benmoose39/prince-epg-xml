Ocean
Ocean is a Euronews program entirely dedicated to problems and challenges of marine life, such as pollution, climate change, protection of underwater systems, sustainable fishing and much more. The program draws viewers attention to the importance of restoring marine biodiversity, the marine species becoming extinct, the uncertain marine future due to acidification of waters, and what can be done by everyone to save the ocean and its flora and fauna.
