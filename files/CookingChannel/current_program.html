Italy's Amalfi Coast
Andrew Zimmern dives into the culinary treasures of Italy's Amalfi Coast; he samples raw red mullet aboard a fishing boat, tastes years-old fish sauce, visits the Sistine Chapel of salumi, and tucks into Mamma Agata's cloud-like meatballs.
