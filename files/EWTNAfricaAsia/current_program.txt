<span Class=" Theme Emphasis">new!</span> Holy Mass With The Blessing And Imposition Of The Ashes
Ash Wednesday Mass from the Basilica of St. Sabina, marking the beginning of Lent. The celebration will begin at St. Anselm with a Lenten Station and Penetential procession to St. Sabina. The Holy Mass and imposition of the ashes will be presided over by Card. Angelo De Donatis, Major Penitentiary and Delegate of the Holy Father. (120 minutes).
