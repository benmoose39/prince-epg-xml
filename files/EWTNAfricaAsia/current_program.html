Ex Corde Ecclesiae
Fr. John Trigilio and Fr. Ken Brighenti explore the lessons learned from John Paul II's writing in "Ex Corde Ecclesiae." Ex Corde Ecclesiae, Latin for "from the heart of the Church" is a guide for Catholic universities and Catholic education in general. (30 minutes).
