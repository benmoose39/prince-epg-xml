Monday Of Holy Week: The Paschal Mystery
Fr. John Cavanagh describes the supper at Bethany and the anointing of Jesus' feet. He meditates on how the cross and resurrection are essential parts of the Paschal mystery of Christ. From the Church of St. David in Pantasaph, Holywell, North Wales. (30 minutes).
