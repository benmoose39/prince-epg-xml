The Word Was Made Flesh

Our Lord And The Samaritan Woman At The Well
Fr. Apostoli relates the dramatic story of the Samaritan woman who encounters Jesus at Jacob's Well. Jesus leads from a discussion of natural water to the deepest need of the human heart, to be filled with God's grace. (30 minutes).
