Live: Bloomberg Real Yield

Not everyone has a lot of money to invest, which is why Bloomberg has financial indices that are based on fixed income, which is also the focus on this weekly show. The program covers news and trends in the fixed-income market and includes interviews with guests from major financial institutions. "Bloomberg Real Yield" incorporates data and analysis from terminal bond experts to help dive into topics that are moving the bond markets in order to help empower viewers to make knowledgeable financial decisions.
