Live: Bloomberg Markets: Asia
In an effort to provide extensive coverage of worldwide financial markets, Bloomberg features several regional shows under the "Bloomberg Markets" banner, with each focusing on that geographic area's markets. This Asian-focused version is hosted by Shery Ahn, David Ingles, Haidi Lun, Rishaad Salamat and Angie Lau. They report the latest business news and speak with the likes of political and business leaders to help investors make investment decisions that build their portfolios.
