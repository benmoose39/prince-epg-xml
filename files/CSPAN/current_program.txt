Peter Slen hosts this weekly interview series. Interviews feature people from a wide range of backgrounds, such as journalists, teachers, politicians, authors, historians, influencers, and technology innovators. These people add context to the day's news and bring new ideas to policy. Interviewees have included David McCullough, Pat Buchanan, David Brooks, Tucker Carlson, John Bolton, Susan Page, House Speaker Nancy Pelosi, and others.
