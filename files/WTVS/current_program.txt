Tales From the D
Host Tino G sits and talks with Johnny "Bee" Badanjek. At 16, Johnny "Bee" Badanjek was the drummer for the Motor City's premier rock and roll band, Mitch Ryder & The Detroit Wheels. There he hammered out one of the three greatest drum breaks in the history of rock, according to Rolling Stone Magazine (On "Devil With A Blue Dress"). Johnny "Bee" performs, "Taking it Back.".
