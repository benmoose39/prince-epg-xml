Joanna Page's Wild Life

Joanna Page's confidence as a trainee volunteer at her local wildlife rescue centre is growing as she helps x-ray the smallest bat she's ever seen with a suspected wing fracture. Joanna also helps Alice look after and finally release some noisy garden birds.
