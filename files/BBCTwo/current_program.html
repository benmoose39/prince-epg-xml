Stockport
The team are at Stockport Town Hall in Greater Manchester, and Paul Martin is joined by experts Mark Stacey and Philip Serrell. Philip spots a pair of charcoal drawings by Northern artist Trevor Grimshaw and Mark spies some gold European coins. Paul also takes time out to visit Manchester Art Gallery to admire the work of Pre-Raphaelite artist Ford Madox Brown.
