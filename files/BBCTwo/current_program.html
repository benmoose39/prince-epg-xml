Chronicles of the Glens

Summer
The Glens are entering what would normally be their busiest season, as visitors come to enjoy the unspoiled beaches and pretty villages that hug the coastline. Covid-19 restrictions have led to the cancellation of village festivals, but there are still ways to have fun, and the Glens' community is used to dealing with adversity.
