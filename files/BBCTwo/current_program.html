World's Busiest Railway 2015

Dan Snow, Anita Rani and Robert Llewellyn examine the role the station plays in long distance travel across India. The country's rail passengers travel 3 million km every day, that's the distance to the moon and back, 4 times. Anita joins passengers on a long distance sleeper across India, to discover who's travelling, what they pay for their tickets and what conditions are like on board.
