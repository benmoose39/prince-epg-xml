Flog It!

Watts Gallery 9
Presented from the Watts Gallery in Surrey, the Arts & Crafts home of renowned artists Mary and George Frederic Watts. Experts David Harper and Natasha Raskin are on hand to find a selection of objects to take to auction. Some wonderful WW1 medals catch Natasha's eye, while David is drawn to a fabulous collection of mid-century posters. Paul Martin uncovers the story of ceramicist and designer Mary Watts with a visit to her fabulous chapel.
