Show Tunes at the BBC: Volume 2

Elaine Paige takes another toe tapping trip through the BBC's archives, rustling up the biggest show tunes from Britain's best-loved musicals. From Les Mis to Joseph, Oklahoma! to Hamilton, there's something for everyone. Featuring stunning turns from some of the biggest names in the business, such as Dame Shirley Bassey, Alfie Boe, Hugh Jackman and Barbara Dickson, to a set of wonderful cast ensembles.
