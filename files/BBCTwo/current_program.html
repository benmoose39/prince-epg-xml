Great British Menu

London and South East England: Judging
The two highest-scoring chefs from London and south east England must go head-to-head and cook their six-course menus again. They need to impress a panel of exacting judges: Michelin-starred chef Tom Kerridge, former Great British Menu Champion of Champions Lorna McNee, and comedian and food podcaster Ed Gamble. The guest judge is archdeacon and women's justice campaigner Mina Smallman. How will she judge dishes celebrating great Britons of the past?
