Wild China

Tides of Change
Ancient tea-growing cultures, traditional seaweed-thatched villages, bird-filled wetlands, rare white dolphins, snake-infested islands and futuristic cities jostle along China's fertile eastern seaboard. The seaboard marks the front-line in the scramble for resources and space between 700 million people and a surprising wealth of wildlife.
