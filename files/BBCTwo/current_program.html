Dom Digs In

Buses
Dom's working on the buses, finding out what it takes to keep the most popular form of public transport moving. Across the country, public transport is an essential lifeline, and people make over four million bus journeys every year, to get to work, get children to school and do the weekly shop, not to mention visit loved ones. His mentor in East Yorkshire is area director Ben Gilligan, who has been working in the transport industry for nearly 20 years.
