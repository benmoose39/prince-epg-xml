Weatherman Walking

The Welsh Coast, Beaumaris to Red Wharf Bay
Weatherman Derek Brockway fires the starting cannon at the Beaumaris Regatta on Anglesey. His 14-mile walk then takes him into the town's heavily fortified castle before he heads onto the Welsh Coast Path, towards Red Wharf Bay.
