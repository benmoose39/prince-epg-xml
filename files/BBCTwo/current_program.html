Watts Gallery 12
Paul Martin is at the Watts Gallery in Surrey, a treasure trove of British art and sculpture, with experts Thomas Plant and David Harper. Thomas values an art deco flapper girl, while a trio of pocket watches impresses David. Paul also visits the heathland of the Devil's Punchbowl to uncover gruesome tales of highway robbery.
