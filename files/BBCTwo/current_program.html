Fake or Fortune?

Landseer
Fiona Bruce and Philip Mould investigate a painting that could be the work of the celebrated artist Sir Edwin Landseer. If it is, they will have rediscovered a much-loved Victorian masterpiece that was thought to have been destroyed by a catastrophic flood almost a century ago. The painting depicts the terrible aftermath of battle with fallen soldiers and horses evoking the horror of war.
