Leicester 17
Paul Martin presents from De Montfort Hall in Leicester. Antiques experts Catherine Southon and Claire Rawle are hunting through the crowds for the finest antiques and collectables to take to auction. Paul also discovers local radio was born in Leicester and explores the shocking story surrounding the arrival of the Ugandan Asian population in the city.
