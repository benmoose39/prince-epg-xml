Live: European Indoor Championship Athletics

Apeldoorn: Day 4
Jeanette Kwakye introduces live coverage from Apeldoorn, Netherlands as eight gold medals are handed out in the evening session on day three of the European Indoors. Amongst the finals is the women's long jump, in which Great Britain's Jazmin Sawyers is the defending champion, while in the men's 60m a Briton may win gold for the first time since Richard Kilty in 2017. Titles are also awarded in the men's triple and high jump and the women's pole vault.
