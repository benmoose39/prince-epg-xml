South Pacific

Ocean of Volcanoes
Millions of years are condensed into an hour revealing unforgettable images of an erupting underwater volcanoes; rivers of lava exploding below the waves; roads and houses buried by molten rivers of rock. From these violent beginnings emerge coral reefs of unparalleled richness supporting large groups of grey reef sharks and giant manta rays.
