Weatherman Walking

The Welsh Coast: Fall Bay to Oxwich
Weatherman Derek Brockway is on Gower, but he ignores the route along Worms Head taken by most walkers. Instead, he sets off along a less well-known stretch of coast, from Fall Bay to Oxwich, about eleven miles away.
