Surgeons: At the Edge of Life

Rebuild and Renew
At St John's Hospital in Livingston, consultant ear, nose and throat surgeon Ashley Hay specialises in some of the most challenging cancer cases: removing tumours from the head and neck. He's joining forces with consultant plastic surgeon Patrick Addison for a complex and unusual case. They're hoping to both save their patient's life and preserve her ability to smile.
