Huddersfield
The team are in Huddersfield in West Yorkshire, and experts Mark Stacey and Christina Trevanion are on hand to find a selection of objects to take to auction. A beautifully crafted clock catches Christina's eye, while Mark is drawn to an unusual piece of Lalique glass. Meanwhile, Paul Martin pays a visit to Nostell Priory, a grand house that reveals a perfect marriage between Georgian architecture and craftsmanship.
