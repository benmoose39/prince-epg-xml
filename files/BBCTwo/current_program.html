Great Continental Railway Journeys

Knin to Split
Michael Portillo's railway adventure reaches the Dalmatian coastal city of Zadar, once an important Venetian trading hub and still a centre for the production of a Venetian delicacy, Maraschino cherry liqueur. In Sibenik, Michael finds one of the first towns in the world to enjoy electric street lighting.
