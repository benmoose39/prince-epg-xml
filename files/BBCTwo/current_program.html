All Roads Lead Home

Stephen Mangan, Sue Perkins and Alison Steadman ditch their maps and compasses and attempt to travel the UK using nature as their only guide. Sue takes charge in Cornwall and leads Stephen and Alison from Bodmin Moor to Cape Cornwall. As amateur navigators, they soon discover that one wrong turn could mean disaster. Along the way, Sue takes to the skies and consults some wise women.
