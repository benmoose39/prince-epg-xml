Yellowstone

Summer
As the snow melts, the extent of Yellowstone is revealed. Grizzly bears teach their young how to hunt fish and when to move out through valleys and grasslands into summer forests and up into the peaks. However, summer here is fickle. August is the only month in the year when it does not snow.
