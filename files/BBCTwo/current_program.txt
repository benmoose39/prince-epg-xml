Live: WA World Indoor Championship Athletics
Gabby Logan introduces coverage of the evening session on day one of the World Athletics Indoor Championships in Nanjing, China. There is a gold medal up for grabs in the men's high jump, where Olympic gold medallist Hamish Kerr, of New Zealand, is the reigning champion. Today's action also includes the semi-finals and final of the men's 60m, won by the USA's Christian Coleman when this championship was staged in Glasgow in 2024.
