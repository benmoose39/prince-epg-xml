Legends of Welsh Sport
Born in Ruabon, Wrexham, and discovered by a north Wales talent scout, Hughes became a sensation at Manchester United before a high-profile move to Barcelona. While his teammate Gary Lineker thrived in Spain, Hughes faltered, facing immense challenges that nearly derailed his career. The turning point came in the 1991 European Cup Winners' Cup final, a match that marked his personal and professional redemption.
