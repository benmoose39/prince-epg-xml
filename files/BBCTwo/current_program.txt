Farm to Feast: Best Menu Wins
In the grand final the last two cooks standing face the ultimate culinary showdown. Revisiting two of the ingredients previously featured in the competition, they're tasked with preparing a formal two-course meal to be served in the castle's grand state dining room, enjoyed by the lord and lady of the house, the two food producers behind the key ingredients and of course, judges Danni and Joris.
