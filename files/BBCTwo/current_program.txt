Patagonia: Earth's Secret Paradise
From the Andes peaks we follow the path of the relentless wind, sweeping east through Patagonia's dry desert. We discover a weird world of maras, giant guinea pigs and desert dwelling penguins, and witness the first faltering steps of baby guanacos, Patagonia's very own camels.
