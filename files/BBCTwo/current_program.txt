This Farming Life
It's shearing time for the Imries, but their minds are on their next big event: a family music festival. Once the farm work is done, it's full steam ahead with preparations. They need this event to make a profit and are relying on last-minute ticket sales on the day. In Dumfries and Galloway, after the shock of losing 300 acres of prime grazing, Nick's neighbour has offered to lease him some land, giving him an idea for a new business venture.
