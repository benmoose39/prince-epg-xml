Expedition with Steve Backshall
Steve Backshall leads a team of elite explorers, including world leading underwater cave explorer Robbie Schmittner, former Royal Marine Aldo Kane and diving camera operator, Katy Fraser, into the wilds of the Yucatan Peninsula in Mexico. Under the vast jungle here is the largest unexplored network of caves in the world, comprising thousands of kilometres of passageways.
