Live: London Marathon
Gabby Logan presents live coverage of the 45th London Marathon as world-class runners join thousands of fundraisers on one of the most inspiring days in the British sporting calendar. Commentary is from Andrew Cotter, Steve Cram, Paula Radcliffe and Baroness Tanni Grey-Thompson, while Abby Cook, JJ Chalmers, Jeanette Kwakye and Sarah Mulkerrins will all be reporting from the 26.2-mile route.
