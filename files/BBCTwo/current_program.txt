Flog It!
Paul Martin and experts Will Axon and Thomas Plant open the gates at London Zoo, in Regent's Park to the lucky locals laden with antiques and collectibles. The items are as unpredictable as ever, from a Ducati camera, a watercolour by Walter Sickert's little brother, to a pair of Zulu spears.
