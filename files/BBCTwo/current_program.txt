London Marathon
Gabby Logan looks at the highlights of the 45th London Marathon, including all the key moments from the elite men's, women's and wheelchair races. Kenyan world record holder Ruth Chepngetich appears the one to beat in the elite women's race, Ethiopian Olympic champion Tamirat Tola is the headline act amongst the men, while Swiss duo Marcel Hug and Catherine Debrunner are vying to retain their wheelchair crowns.
