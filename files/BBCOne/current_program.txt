The Interview
Acclaimed author Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie talks to Emma Barnett about her childhood, her powerful storytelling and exploration of themes such as identity, feminism and culture. Adichie's groundbreaking works, including Half of a Yellow Sun and Americanah, have captivated readers worldwide. Adichie delves into her literary journey, personal experiences and the inspirations behind her writing.
