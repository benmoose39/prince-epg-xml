Money for Nothing

At Wallyford Recycling Centre in East Lothian, upcycler extraordinaire Sarah Moore is on the lookout for two unloved items she can save and sell on for a profit. To help transform her skip finds, Sarah turns to designer Lucy Tiffney, who has a pair of old lamps to contend with.
