Thelma Schoonmaker
Thelma Schoonmaker has, for over five decades, been Martin Scorsese's cutting room collaborator. Having edited his first feature film in 1967, she has worked on every Scorsese movie since Raging Bull. Thelma Schoonmaker has won three Academy Awards.
