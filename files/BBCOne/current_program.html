Homes Under the Hammer

A large home in Barry in south Wales, a terrace in Nuneaton in Warwickshire and a quirky one-bedroom flat in a Victorian former schoolhouse on the Isle of Wight are sold at auction. Martin, Martel and Jacqui meet the new owners and hear their plans for their investment projects.
