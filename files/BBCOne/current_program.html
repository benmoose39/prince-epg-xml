Clive Myrie's Caribbean Adventure

La Vega - Dominican Republic
For 11 months of the year, the city of La Vega is well off the tourist track. But each February, it bursts into life. Over the course of four weekends, it's loud, hot and colourful as visitors from all over the world arrive to join in the famous street carnivals.
