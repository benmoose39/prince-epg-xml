Money for Nothing

At Woking Recycling Centre in Surrey, Sarah Moore is on the lookout for two items she can salvage, spruce up and sell on for a profit. She enlists the help of designer-maker Daniel Heath to give new life to a retro bureau, and for her own project, Sarah gets creative with three wooden doors.
