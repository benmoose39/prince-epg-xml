Dream Holiday Left Me with Gastric Infection
Gloria Hunniford, Julia Somerville and Louise Minchin tackle viewers' travel issues - including the cases of holidaymakers who suffered from debilitating gastric infections following their holiday to Cape Verde. We meet the law firm taking on their claims. Also on the programme, the customers left out of pocket when they were unable to get a holiday refund following the death of their loved ones. And the team investigate the booking error that cost one airline passenger ?4,000.
