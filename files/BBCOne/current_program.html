Rip-Off Britain

I Cancelled My Card, but Scammers Kept Spending
Gloria Hunniford, Julia Somerville and Louise Minchin investigate the mysterious transactions made via mobile phone payments that happened after the customer had cancelled their bank card. Experts explain what can be done to protect customers. Also on the programme, the small business owners being blackmailed on social media with fake reviews, the fraudulent websites taking clean air zone payments, and the new technology designed to help us spot scams.
