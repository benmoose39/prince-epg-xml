Money for Nothing

Expert upcycler Jacqui Joseph is at Earlswood recycling centre, scouring the skips for two items that she can rework to reveal their true potential. To help turn drab into fab, Jacqui enlists luggage legend Neil Wragg to work on some sofa leather, while Jacqui's creativity is tested by a mannequin.
