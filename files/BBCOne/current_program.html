Money for Nothing

Sarah Moore is at Wallyford Recycling Centre in East Lothian, hoping to unearth some quality discards before they hit the rubbish heap. With the help of bag maker Neil Wragg and screen-print artist Daniel Heath, Sarah hopes to breathe new life into a leaking tent, a scandi style coffee table and a miniature greenhouse. But will she harvest any profits by selling them on?
