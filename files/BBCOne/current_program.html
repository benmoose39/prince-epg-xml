Alan Sugar
Lord Alan Sugar, a man famed for his straight-talking and no-nonsense approach to interviews, sits down with Amol Rajan to discuss the future of Britain and his own incredible career. Arguably the UK's most famous rags to riches story, he was the working-class kid from Hackney who went on to become a billionaire with a seat in the Lords and his face all over television.
