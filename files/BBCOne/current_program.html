Escape to the Country

Kent
In his home county of Kent, Steve Brown helps two newlyweds from London find a new spacious and dog-friendly family home in the countryside. Steve also meets another couple who, eight years ago, swapped their city flat for a beautiful cottage in the county, where they also established a business.
