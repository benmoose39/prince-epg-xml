My Life

On the Fast Track
Sandro is a 14-year-old boy with a driving ambition to take part in the Le Mans 24-hour race. His big dream may be a few years off, but he is determined to make it happen. Sandro is a keen kart racer when we meet him, and we follow him as he pursues his dream to graduate to racing cars on professional circuits. We see him in kart-racing action, and are with him as he learns the enormity of what lies ahead if he is to achieve his dream of becoming a Formula One racing driver.
