Operation Ouch!
Identical twin doctors Dr Chris and Dr Xand van Tulleken show how incredible the human body is. With big stunts and exciting experiments, the doctors explore the extraordinary ways our bodies work and show how medicine can fix us when things go wrong. And in a new mind-bending strand, the medical twins test out psychological tricks on an unsuspecting public. The doctors look at your kidneys and reveal how these amazing cleaning machines work. The ouch-mobile is open for more intriguing medical mysteries, and Dr Xand tries out some vision-shifting spectacles for this episode's mind-benders. Meanwhile, in accident and emergency, one patient has an unusual break in his leg and another has a stone stuck in his ear.
