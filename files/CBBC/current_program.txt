Dragons
Animated series based on How to Train Your Dragon, continuing the adventures of Hiccup and his dragon Toothless. Can Hiccup and the people of Berk truly live in harmony with the dragons? Snotlout is concerned when his dragon's flame goes out, and the kids have to figure out how to reignite it before it's too late.
