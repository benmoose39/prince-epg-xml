Deadly 60
In this Deadly 60 special, Steve Backshall takes a look at some of the super skills and senses that animals around the world use to hunt and survive. These range from the blistering speed and acceleration of the cheetah to the elastic tongue and 360-degree vision of the chameleon and even a shark with a remarkable hidden sixth sense.
