Dragons
Animated series based on How to Train Your Dragon, continuing the adventures of Hiccup and his dragon Toothless. Can Hiccup and the people of Berk truly live in harmony with the dragons? Once every ten years, a mysterious glowing dragon visits Berk. This year, Hiccup and Astrid are determined to find out where it's coming from - and stop it from reaching the village.
