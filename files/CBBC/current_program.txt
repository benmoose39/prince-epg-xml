Deadly Dinosaurs with Steve Backshall
Steve Backshall's on a mission to find the most dangerous dinosaur of all time. Using the latest tech and epic experiments, he'll do whatever it takes to crown his king of the killers! His search sees him chomp through a car like a tyrannosaurus rex, wield an axe like an allosaurus, before he hitches a ride in the biggest tank in Britain to showcase the incredible size and power of the spinosaurus! But which of these immense predators will come out on top.
