DIY Deadly with Steve Backshall
In this episode, Steve delves into the lives of the animals that live in our seas and on our coasts. He unravels the mystery of a curled-up British shark and a squid egg mass. In a DIY demo, Steve then shows us his secret treasure chest, which includes the most venomous animal in the oceans, a fossilised shark tooth as well as one from a sperm whale.
