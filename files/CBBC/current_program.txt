Danger Mouse
Baron Greenback brews a plot to steal every beverage on the planet and mix them all together, making an undrinkable, disgusting sludge. By depriving the population of their much-needed drinks break he'll cause worldwide exhaustion. Danger Mouse seems unfazed, but without his cherished tea Penfold wilts on their rescue mission. Can Danger Mouse thwart the baron's thirsty plot.
