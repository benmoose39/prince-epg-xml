Super Happy Magic Forest
The heroes find treasure as they bid to save Merhaven from an angry seaweed. Blossom is delighted with her bath bomb treasure, but rather than use it, she insists on bringing it along on the watery quest to save it for a special occasion. But as the angry seaweed nears Merhaven, Blossom realises she must finally sacrifice her treasure to save the day.
