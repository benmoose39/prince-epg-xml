Blow Your Mind
Dr Chris and Dr Xand van Tulleken track down the most awesome, incredible things in the universe! This time it's all about space. In this episode they find out about the length of our days and how they are getting longer - and that it's all because of the moon. Their friend, space scientist Dr Maggie, discovers some clever coral that can tell the time and their speedy chum Kate Humble drives a car at over a thousand miles an hour - amazing.
