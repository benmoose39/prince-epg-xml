Existential U-Turn: Chris's Journey
As a young officer in the German Army, Chris was on track for a what he thought would be success in life, one in which there was no room for God. However, some unexpected circumstances and a dramatic event on the other side of the globe started challenging his worldview and soon brought about a total change of direction.
