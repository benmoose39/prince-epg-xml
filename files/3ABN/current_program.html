The Ultimate Gift
In this episode of Digging Deeper, Pastor Ryan Johnson is joined by Pastors John Dinzey and Gordon Fraser to explore the profound significance of the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. They delve into the foundational truths of Christianity, discussing the sacrificial love of Jesus, His victory over sin and death, and the hope of eternal life for believers.
