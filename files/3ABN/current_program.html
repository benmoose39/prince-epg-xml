The Judge, The Widow and the Power of Persistent
The parable of the unjust judge and the widow was given by Christ to highlight the necessity of persistent prayer; the unjust judge is portrayed as reluctantly, but eventually, coming to the aid of a widow after her persistent pleas for justice.
