Sanctuary Salvation & Our Savior

The Day of Atonement
Yom Kippur is a day in which the sanctuary is cleansed of the record of forgiven sins of Israel; the Bible tells in Hebrews Chapter 8 that the sanctuary and its services is a type of the sanctuary in heaven where Jesus ministers as high priest.
