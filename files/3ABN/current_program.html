A Wolf in Sheep's Clothing

From Heavy Metal to Heavenly Melodies: Davie Garcia's Redemption Journey
Delving deep into the life of Davie Garcia, a young man lured into the world of Wicca through the captivating sounds of Heavy Metal music; with an exploration into the biblical connection between music and spiritual influence, Davie recounts his turbulent journey from the allure of the occult to finding solace and salvation in Christ.
