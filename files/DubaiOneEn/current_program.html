Little Women

A movie adaptation of the classic English novel, Little Women retells the timeless story of the March sisters. Set in the post-Civil War era, the elegant Meg, feisty Jo, ambitious Amy and meek Beth will make you laugh and cry with them as they take on the world to live life on their own terms.
