Stadium Lockup
Protecting this stadium are highly trained men and women from law enforcement, private security teams and medics. In this episode, tensions are at an all-time high between these rivaling fans when a fight quickly erupts requiring multiple personnel to break it up. The medical team responds to different calls of fans who have passed out while the security team must handle a curious accusation of a younger fan assaulting an older gentleman. Fortunately, the video tape does not lie.
